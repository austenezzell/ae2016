BeardseasonUX / UI / Art Direction / Dev

Beardseason is a nonprofit charity turning beards into life saving conversation starters, challenging you, your friends and family to become an Ambassador and have a #BeardSeasonSkincheck. This project is the brainchild of Jimmy Niggles aka Scott Maggs. Jimmy is on a quest to spread Melanoma awareness and in doing so, is saving lives on the way. Beardseason.com is on it's third iteration. The site now features ambassadors who have pledged to get a skin check and spread awareness, a tool to help users find clinics in their area, and tips for helping spot bad moles.

My role: UX / UI / Dev Beardseason.com
